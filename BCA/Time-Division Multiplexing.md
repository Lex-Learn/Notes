- Time-division multiplexing, data is transmitted at a specific slot of time.
- User's messages are buffered at the receiver end for a complete frame.

![](../Pictures/fi2.png)


## Advantages and disadvantages:
- #### Advantages
	- Single link is used.
	- Doesn't require precise carrier matching.
	- Low cost of adding a user.
	- Channel capacity uses is high.
	- No need to identify each traffic packet.
	
- #### Disadvantages 
	- Complex technique.
	- Initial cost is high.
	- Sensitive to other user and cause problem.
